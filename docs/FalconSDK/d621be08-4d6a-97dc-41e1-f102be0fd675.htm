Remarks To prevent replay attacks it is important that the last valid sequence numbers of all communication partners are kept up-to date and persisted across runs. The former is handled automatically by Falcon's Bus object. For the latter, it is advised to call the SaveDeviceSequenceCounters method regularly to save the current sequence counters to a file.
